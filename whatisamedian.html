What is a median?

Median

The middle, or central value in an ordered set of numbers

Examples

What is the median of 1, 2, 3, 5, 8?
Answer: 3

What is the median of 2, 4, 6, 8?
Answer: \( \frac{4+6}{2} = \frac{10}{2} = 5 \)
