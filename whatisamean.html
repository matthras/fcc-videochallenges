What is a mean?

Mean

  • Colloquially known as the 'average'
  • A measure of central tendenacy
  • Referred to as the 'expectation' or 'expected value' in advanced mathematics

Calculating the Mean

Sum all numbers, then divide the total by the number of numbers

e.g. What is the mean of 1, 4, 9, and 16?
Calculation: \( \frac{1+4+9+16}{4} = \frac{30}{4} = 7.5 \)
